About Me


Puspa Raj Dhakal

Professional Lecturer

Puspa Raj Dhakal has been in the education sector for a very long time. From a very young age he started to teach school students to make money for his own studies. According to him, he taught more than 8 schools from gorkha and kathmandu. At the age of 22, he was a lecturer at B.S.T college. After that he mentioned teaching in Mahendra Ratna campus and Kritipur campus. He further pursued his career as a government officer. Firstly he served 1 year in Educational Training Center, Bara. Then he was transfered to Educational Training Center, Tanahun and served there for 8 years. He worked at Education Training Center, Dhulikhel for a year and later Curriculam Development Center, Sano Thimi.

As an district education officer, he worked at Education Development and Coordination Unit, Bhaktapur. Further more he served as Under Secretary (Personal Assistant) of Honorable Minister of Education, Science and Technology, Singha Durbar. Recently, he is working as education head in Madi Municipality, Chitwan. In spite of this he teaches students appearing in Public Service Commission in part time. He along with his wife (government officer) have written many books as a reference material for people appearing in Public Service Commission Examination. Not only that he has represented Nepal in many programs of China, US, Thailand, Singapore, Japan, Indonesia, India, Malaysia.
